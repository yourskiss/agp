  • CNG

AG&P Pratham’s CNG brings happiness and value to every vehicle owner. It is a cost-effective fuel that is much cheaper than Petrol and Diesel. To promote savings further, CNG boosts engine productivity and gives a higher mileage. The non-corrosive property of this fuel keeps the health of your engine and pipes at the optimum level. By using CNG, you can even contribute to helping the environment as it causes less pollution in the air. Low maintenance costs, improved engine performance, better travel experience, and higher returns from commercial usage are a few reasons why you should have a CNG equipped vehicle.

With the lightness and high ignition temperature of this fuel comes the security and safety of the gas dispersing before a fatal explosion in case of breakage, leakage, or accident. With CNG, extend your journeys and make them safer for yourself and your loved ones
