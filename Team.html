  • Abhilesh Gupta

    Managing Director and CEO

    The CEO of AG&P Pratham comes with an experience of 27 years in the commercial finance industry across the LNG, city gas, and power domains in India and Singapore. A B.Com graduate from Rajasthan University, he has worked with top names in the industry post completion of his education. He was the Group CFO of Asia Genco.; he was a part of Petronet LNG as the GM, Finance. He has also worked at IGL, Delhi’s city gas company in finance.

    View profile

  • Maurice Allgrove

    Director

    Maurice Allgrove is the Senior Vice President, LNG Terminals and Logistics at AG&P. He handles the developments, construction, and procurement for facilities abroad. His work entails handling port projects, liquid bulk, LNG export, and LNG bunkering terminal developments in the U.S., Canada, Latin America, the Caribbean, and Indonesia. He is an experienced port, marine, and geotechnical engineer with expertise in business development and project execution. In his former role As Vice President of Ports and Marine Terminals and Transportation for Worley, he managed large multi-disciplinary teams working on major projects in the upstream, midstream, and downstream oil and gas sectors, gas-to-power operations, and petrochemicals plants. He was previously the Project Manager for the 170,000 m3 FSRU terminal in Cartagena, Colombia. His degrees include Civil Engineering from the University of the West Indies and a master's degree from McGill University, Montreal, Canada. With his expertise in structuring geotechnical engineering, Mr. Allgrove has developed business and executed projects during his tenure at these firms.

    View profile

  • Rituraj Mathur

    Director

    Rituraj Mathur is Senior Vice President, Corporate at AG&P. He is responsible for building AG&P’s new businesses and strengthening the company’s global gas expansion strategy. Before joining AG&P, Rituraj worked at the International Monetary Fund (IMF) in Washington D.C, USA, advising Latin American and Middle Eastern governments on fiscal and monetary policies. Rituraj has extensive experience in international investments, capital-raising, and commercial negotiations. He returned to AG&P following a successful stint as CEO of his family business in the UAE.
