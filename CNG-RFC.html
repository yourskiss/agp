Retro Fitment Centers

Convinced about the benefits of CNG and if you want to immediately convert your vehicle to CNG please do read about the points you need to understand before making the decision.

CNG Kit

CNG Kit

  • Always choose an RTO-approved CNG kit and retrofitter.
  • Make sure the CNG kit is compatible with your vehicle.
  • Do check the quality of the accessories used.
  • Make sure your CNG kit is fitted correctly.
Cylinder

Cylinder

  • The cylinder should be properly tested and certified before installation.
  • Do not opt for secondhand or pre-used cylinders.
  • Regularly test your cylinder for leaks.
RC and Insurance

RC & Insurance

  • The local RTO must endorse your RC book and the fuel type changed.
  • Notify your insurance provider about the change.

Interested in converting to CNG?

If you want to begin your journey with CNG, visit an RTO-approved retro fitment center and get your kit installed.
