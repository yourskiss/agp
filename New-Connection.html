DOCUMENTS REQUIRED

Are you ready to switch to natural gas? Follow the steps below and start your journey with PNG and CNG, today.

  • PNG for Households
  • PNG for Commercial Use
PNG for Households

  • You can directly fill our application form or call us to start your clean fuel journey with AG&P Pratham

  • While filling the form, you will have to ensure that you have all the documents for KYC verification.

  • Once verified, your application moves to the next step where we send our team for feasibility study

  • After feasibility study, plumbing, gas meter and other equipment necessary to start the supply of gas will be installed

  • We will now help you convert the burner suitable for Piped Natural Gas

  • Once the installation is complete, the gas will be supplied to your homes. Rest assured, we will be there at every step!

PNG for Households

  • Society Share Certificate
  • Possession Letter
PNG for Commercial Use

  • The first step is to initiate the process. You can either fill our online form or call us with your request for a connection.

  • Once the request is filed, a feasibility study will be conducted by our technical team.

  • Upon receiving complete documents and KYC verification, we will then prepare a proposal for your acceptance and payment.

  • After that, we will guide you through civil work, installing internal pipelines and choose PNG-compatible burner

  • AG&P Pratham team will then install the pipelines and the gas meter.

  • Once everything is in place, we will ensure the proper functioning to let you enjoy the benefits of piped natural gas.
