WHY CHOOSE PNG

PNG as an alternative offers many benefits. Below are some of the reasons why one should consider using PNG.

  • PNG
    LPG

  • Eco friendly

    PNG is a cleaner burning fuel and is mostly methane, which makes it eco-friendly.
    LPG is less eco-friendly as it is mostly propane or butane (bottles gas).

  • Safer

    It is lighter than air and dissipates quickly in case of leak or breakage.
    It is not lighter than air and expands 250 times in case of leak.

  • 24-hour supply

    Supply to the stove can be controlled or turned off through the valve.
    Controlling the supply to the stove is not as easy.

  • Cost effective

    It is economical with lower rates and installation charges.
    Has a higher rate with cylinder security charges and booking hassles

  • Easy supply

    PNG gas stoves have nozzles with bigger diameter for a better supply.
    The nozzles have a smaller diameter for a slower supply.

  • Leakage Identification

    It is an odourless gas but to ensure safety and detection, a little smell is added.
    It has a pungent odour.

  • Convenient

    Since it cannot be stored, it is supplied through a direct pipeline that constantly delivers fuel to your house.
    It needs to be stored thus comes in cylinders that need to be pre-booked.

One gas, many uses!

PNG, which is supplied for commercial use to restaurants, hotels, schools, hospitals, clinics, crematoriums, temples, churches, gurudwaras, etc., is a versatile gas that can be used for more than one purpose.
