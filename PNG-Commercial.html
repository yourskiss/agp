WHY CHOOSE PNG

PNG as an alternative offers many benefits. Below are some of the reasons why one should consider using PNG.

  • Eco-Friendly

    PNG is a cleaner burning fuel and is mostly methane, which makes it eco-friendly. LPG is not as eco-friendly as it is mostly propane or butane (bottles gas).

  • Safety

    PNG is lighter than air and dissipates quickly in case of leak or breakage, as compared to LPG.

  • Control

    PNG Supply to the stove can be controlled or turned off through the valve while controlling the supply of LPG to the stove is not as easy.

  • Economical

    PNG is economical with lower rates and installation charges while LPG has a higher rate with cylinder security charges and booking hassles.

  • Supply type

    PNG is supplied through a direct pipeline that ensures constant delivery while LPG needs to be stored thus comes in cylinders that need to be pre-booked.

  • Odour

    PNG is an odourless gas but to ensure safety and detection, a little smell is added while LPG has Pungent odour.

  • Usage

    PNG gas stoves have nozzles with bigger diameter for a better supply while LPG nozzles have a smaller diameter for a slower supply.

  • Payment

    PNG bills are paid monthly depending on your usage while LPG needs to be pre-paid to get the cylinder installed.
