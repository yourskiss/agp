AG&P Pratham pipeline is the answer to all your domestic fuel issues. We provide you with clean fuel that is directly supplied to your home and is safe for your family as well as the environment. The direct connection ensures a break from the surprise of your cylinder running out, the tedious task of booking fuel delivery, and maintaining an extra space for a spare. Upon one call, our team reaches your area to connect you to a continuous supply of natural gas. The multi-layered safety measures and guidelines followed by the installation team prevent any leakage or spillage.

Switch to a fuel that is healthier to use, easier to install, and much more affordable. With PNG, we ensure undisturbed cooking and celebrations.
