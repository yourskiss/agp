  • About AG&P Pratham

AG&P Pratham, India, the City Gas Distribution (CGD) arm and a critical pillar of Singapore based AG&P Group is a leading LNG and Gas distribution company. With the help of advanced technical support and a seamless logistics system, we successfully supply environment and customer friendly fuelling solutions 24/7 for everyday use at homes, offices, plants, and on the road.

As one of the largest international gas suppliers, we have taken natural gas to 33 districts across Rajasthan, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka. With a CGD network that spreads across 278,000 square kilometres, our gas pipelines cover 17,000 inch-km. The next step is to establish 1500 new CNG stations in 8 years.

We are determined to create a sustainable fuel system in India by promoting the use of natural gas. We are closely working with local communities across the country to create awareness around the economic and environmental benefits of using PNG and CNG, for personal as well as commercial purposes, thus, inviting them to be a part of India's net-zero carbon footprint by 2070 mission.

OUR CGD NETWORKS

We are not just helping our customers generate more business and cut costs, but also creating employment opportunities in the country.

AG&P Pratham is building a significant network of Foreign Direct Investment in India. We have brought technical expertise and deep energy mastery to the country to increase employment opportunities.  Locals are being trained in skills of the trade and operational distinction to become a part of our CGD Network.
