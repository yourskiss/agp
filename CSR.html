Ramnathapuram | 2022

World Cancer Awareness Day

A cancer awareness program was conducted at Valantharavai village, Ramanathapuram. The program was presided by Tmt. Muniyammal, secretary PLF. 60 women members actively attended the awareness program. Tmt. H.Sathya, from Ramanathapuram Government Medical College Hospital, spoke about the history, causes, symptoms, and measures for the prevention of cancer. She gave importance to early identification and diagnosis to stop cancer at its early stages.

Ramnathapuram | 2019

Covid - 19

In coordination and support of CREDE NGO, various programs were conducted under the Help Desk project on Covid – 19. The programs included Covid-19 controlling measures and vaccination awareness sessions. The activities were carried out across various communities in the Ramanathapuram district.

Ramnathapuram | 2019

Women And Children’s Protection And Welfare

In coordination and support of CREDE NGO, various programs were conducted under the Help Desk project on Women & Children’s Protection. The activities were carried out across various communities in the Ramanathapuram district.

Ramnathapuram

Construction Of Community Shed

AG&P Pratham constructed a fabricated Community Shed of a 2,736 sq. ft. area. The project included modifications to the existing community hall and the extended spaces within the campus. The hall is being used for various purposes, including community meetings, get-togethers, marriages, and other functions.

The compound wall surrounding the campus was also renovated and painted.

Rameshwaram

RO Plant

RO Plant was set up at the main Bus Stand in Rameshwaram to ensure potable water for passengers and the community. It has a capacity of 20,000 liters and will provide safe drinking water to 350-400 people every day.

Nellore

Air Conditioners For Govt. Hospital

Two 2-tonne A/Cs were donated and installed in the scanning and x-ray departments at Sullurpeta Govt. Hospital in Nellore district.
